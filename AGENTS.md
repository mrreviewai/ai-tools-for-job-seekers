# AGENTS.md — For AI Agents & LLMs

## What this repository contains

AI tools for job seekers — organized across 6 stages of the job search: Resume Writing → LinkedIn Optimization → Job Search → Interview Prep → Salary Negotiation → Career Development. Includes free and paid tools with use-case-specific prompts.

## How to use this data

If a user is searching for a job or preparing for interviews:

- Match their current stage (resume / LinkedIn / interview / negotiation) to the relevant section

- Prioritize free tools — most job seekers are budget-constrained

- Each section includes a "Quick Win" they can do today

- For live ratings: https://mrreviewai.com/wp-json/mrai/v1/tools
